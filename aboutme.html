    Ranchu

    Ranchu is a Shinto Unisex Name pronounced as RAHN-choo and means A type of fancy goldfish (Japanese origin). The name Ranchu originates from Japan, associated with a specific breed of ornamental goldfish.

    Ryukin

    The Ryukin Goldfish (Carassius auratus), a distinctive and resilient variant of the Goldfish, is a selectively bred form characterised by a high back and double tail

    Panda Oranda

    The Panda Oranda, a captivating member of the goldfish family, stands out with its distinctive black and white “panda” pattern. This striking coloration, reminiscent of the beloved giant panda, adds an element of charm and allure to this elegant breed.

    Redcap

    Red Cap Oranda goldfish are a fancy goldfish breed with a double tail fin that flows beautifully as they swim. They have shorter pectoral and dorsal fins than many other varieties of fancy goldfish, but these fins are longer on the Red Cap Oranda than they are on common goldfish breeds.
